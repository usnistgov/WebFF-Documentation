10.1. Langevin Dissipative¶

10.1.1. Functional Form¶

The Langevin dissipative potential has the functional form:

\(E=\gamma\)

The force-field parameters for this potential and units are given by:

Equation Symbol

Parameter Definition

Units

\(\gamma\)

Langevin thermostat

mass/time
