8.9. Non-Bond Energy Renormalization Potential¶

8.9.1. Functional Form¶

The non-bond Energy Renormalization potential has the functional form:

\(E=\left( {{\epsilon }_{A}}-{{\epsilon }_{g}} \right)\left[ \frac{1}{1+{{e}^{-k\left( T-{{T}_{T}} \right)}}} \right]+{{\epsilon }_{g}}\)

The force-field parameters for this potential and units are given by:

Equation Symbol

Parameter Definition

Units

\({\epsilon }_{A}\)

Epsilon value in Arrhenius regime

energy/mol

\({\epsilon }_{g}\)

Epsilon value in glassy regime

energy/mol

\(k\)

Temperature breadth of the transition

N/A

\({T}_{T}\)

Crossover point of sigmoidal function

temperature
