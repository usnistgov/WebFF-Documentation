5.7. Quadratic Dihedral¶

5.7.1. Functional Form¶

The quadratic dihedral potential has the functional form:

\(E = {K_{d,ijkl}} \cdot \left( {{\phi_{ijkl}} - {\phi_{0,ijkl}}} \right)^2\)

The force-field parameters for this potential and units are given by:

Equation Symbol

Parameter Definition

Units

\(K_{d,ijkl}\)

Dihedral coefficient for atoms [i,j,k,l]

energy/degrees^2

\(\phi_{0,ijkl}\)

Equilibrium Dihedral for atoms [i,j,k,l]

degrees
