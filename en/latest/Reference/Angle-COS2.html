4.3. COS2 Angle¶

4.3.1. Functional Form¶

The COS2 angle potential has the functional form:

\(E={{K}_{a,ijk}}{{\left[ \cos \left( {{\theta }_{ijk}} \right)-\cos \left( {{\theta }_{0,ijk}} \right) \right]}^{2}}\)

The force-field parameters for this potential and units are given by:

Equation Symbol

Parameter Definition

Units

\(K_{a,ijk}\)

Angle coefficient for atoms [i,j,k]

energy

\(\theta_{0,ijk}\)

Equilibrium angle for atoms [i,j,k]

degrees
