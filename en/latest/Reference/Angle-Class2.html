4.2. Class2 Angle¶

4.2.1. Functional Form¶

The class 2 angle potential has the functional form:

\(E={{K}_{2,ijk}}{{\left( {{\theta }_{ijk}}-{{\theta }_{0,ijk}} \right)}^{2}}+{{K}_{3,ijk}}{{\left( {{\theta }_{ijk}}-{{\theta }_{0,ijk}} \right)}^{3}}+{{K}_{4,ijk}}{{\left( {{\theta }_{ijk}}-{{\theta }_{0,ijk}} \right)}^{4}}\)

The force-field parameters for this potential and units are given by:

Equation Symbol

Parameter Definition

Units

\(K_{2,ijk}\)

Angle coefficient for atoms [i,j,k] (quadratic term)

energy/degrees^2

\(K_{3,ijk}\)

Angle coefficient for atoms [i,j,k] (cubic term)

energy/degrees^3

\(K_{4,ijk}\)

Angle coefficient for atoms [i,j,k] (quartic term)

energy/degrees^4

\(\theta_{0,ijk}\)

Equilibrium angle for atoms [i,j,k]

degrees
